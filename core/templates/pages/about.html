Our Story

PlayR was born out of a passion for sports and the desire to make it easier for everyone to play. Like many of you, we love the spontaneity and community of pickup games. That's where PlayR comes in. We're building this platform to remove those organizational headaches and let you focus on what truly matters: enjoying the game and the camaraderie with fellow players.

Meet the Creator

Colin Varney

Hi there! I'm the creator of PlayR. My passion for soccer started over 15 years ago, and ever since, I've been deeply involved in the sport as a player, and sometimes organizer, in various pickup leagues and friendly games.

Through countless weekends spent on the field, I've experienced firsthand the joy of pickup games and also the challenges of getting them organized. From uneven teams to players feeling left out, I saw a real need for a tool that could streamline the process and make pickup games more enjoyable for everyone.

That's why I decided to build PlayR. My goal is to create a user-friendly platform that empowers group organizers to effortlessly manage their games, randomize teams fairly, and ensure that every player gets a chance to participate. I believe everyone deserves the opportunity to experience the fun and benefits of pickup sports, and I hope this tool can help make that happen in communities everywhere.

Dedicated to making pickup games better for everyone.
